About the Author

Meet the creator of this website

Ibrahim

Student - IT Department

I am Ibrahim, a student from Hama, Syria. I created this website as part of my project to share the beauty and rich history of my hometown with the world.

Hama is a city that holds a special place in my heart. Growing up surrounded by the ancient norias, the flowing Orontes River, and the warm hospitality of its people has shaped who I am today. Through this website, I hope to give others a glimpse of what makes Hama such a remarkable city.

This project was created using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as part of the Final Project Assignment.

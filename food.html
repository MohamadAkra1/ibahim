Local Food

The traditional flavors of Hama

Famous Dishes

Hama's cuisine reflects the rich agricultural heritage of the region

Kibbeh

Ground meat mixed with bulgur wheat and spices, prepared as "Kibbeh Labanieh" with yogurt or fried as crispy balls.

Fattet Hummus

Layered dish with crispy bread, chickpeas, yogurt, and tahini. Popular at special occasions and family gatherings.

Maqluba

Layers of rice, vegetables, and meat cooked together and flipped for a beautiful presentation.

Shawarma

Thinly sliced meat served in warm bread with garlic sauce, pickles, and fresh vegetables.

Grilled Skewers

Traditional Syrian BBQ with perfectly grilled meat skewers seasoned with local spices.

Syrian Sweets

Baklava, maamoul, and halawet el-jibn. Essential treats for celebrations and hospitality.

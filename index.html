About Hama

Hama is one of the oldest continuously inhabited cities in the world, with a history spanning over 8,000 years.

Location

Hama is located in western-central Syria, on the banks of the Orontes River. It is the capital of Hama Governorate and the fourth largest city in Syria.

Rich History

From ancient Ebla to the Roman era, through the Islamic golden age to modern times, Hama has been a crossroads of civilizations for millennia.

Culture & Heritage

Famous for its traditional souks, Ottoman architecture, and the iconic norias (water wheels) that have been turning for centuries.
